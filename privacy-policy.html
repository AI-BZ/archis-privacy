Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ARCHIS Music Label ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our use of the YouTube API Services and our YouTube channels.

1. Service Description

ARCHIS Music Label operates multiple YouTube channels that publish original music content, including music videos, compilation mixes, and short-form content. We use the YouTube Data API v3 to upload and manage our own content across our channels.

2. Information We Collect

Our application does not collect, store, or process any personal information from YouTube users or viewers. Specifically:

  • We do not access any third-party user data.
  • We do not collect viewer information, comments data, or analytics about individual users.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies on any website.

3. Use of YouTube API Services

We use the YouTube Data API v3 solely for managing our own channels and content. Our API usage includes:

  • Uploading original music videos and compilations to our channels.
  • Updating video metadata (titles, descriptions, tags) in multiple languages.
  • Managing playlists on our channels.
  • Uploading subtitle/caption files for accessibility.

Our use of the YouTube API Services is subject to the YouTube API Services Terms of Service.

4. Google User Data

Our application uses OAuth 2.0 to authenticate with YouTube on behalf of our own channel accounts. We do not request or access any Google user data beyond what is necessary to manage our own YouTube channels. OAuth tokens are stored securely on our local server and are never shared with third parties.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. Our application operates exclusively for internal content management purposes.

6. Data Security

OAuth credentials and API keys are stored securely on our private server with restricted access. We follow industry-standard security practices to protect our credentials and application data.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

8. Google's Privacy Policy

When using our YouTube channels, users are also subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

9. Revoking Access

You can revoke our application's access to your Google account at any time via Google Security Settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: archis.label@gmail.com
